Happy Ghast Pro expands the Happy Ghast experience with useful, vanilla-friendly features for travel, storage, and companion management.

This mod makes Happy Ghasts easier to use in survival by adding tools to recall them, park them, carry items, and temporarily boost their movement speed using snowballs.

Features
Ghast Whistle

Use the Ghast Whistle to recall your Happy Ghast when it floats away.

This helps you quickly bring back your Happy Ghast during exploration, building, or travel.

Ghast Anchor

Use the Ghast Anchor to set a home or parking spot for your Happy Ghast.

This keeps your Happy Ghast near a chosen location, such as your base, sky dock, or travel station.

Happy Ghast Saddlebag

Add extra storage to your Happy Ghast with a saddlebag inventory.

This lets your Happy Ghast carry items while you explore, build, mine, or travel long distances.

Snowball Speed Boost

Feed your Happy Ghast a snowball to give it a temporary movement speed boost.

This keeps the feature simple and vanilla-friendly while making Happy Ghast travel feel smoother and more useful.

Why use this mod?

Happy Ghasts are fun flying companions, but they can be hard to manage. Happy Ghast Pro adds practical upgrades without making them feel overpowered.

With this mod, you can:

Recall your Happy Ghast
Park it at a fixed location
Give it portable storage
Boost its speed with snowballs
Make Happy Ghasts better for survival travel
Perfect for

Survival players, builders, explorers, server players, and anyone who wants Happy Ghasts to be more useful while still feeling like part of vanilla Minecraft.
